Education / Career

ESIEE Paris

2022-2027

I studied at ESIEE Paris for five years. I first completed two years of an integrated preparatory program, where I discovered the fundamentals of computer science and electronics while continuing to build my knowledge of mathematics and physics.
After these two preparatory years, I joined the Computer Science and Applications apprenticeship program (the reason I chose this school) for its generalist curriculum, which let me explore a variety of fields, including 3D development with Unity.
That same year, I joined KNDS France Training to further deepen my expertise in 3D.

KNDS France Training

2024-2027

I worked at KNDS France Training for 3 years.
During this time, I developed strong Unity skills through various professional, academic and personal projects.
